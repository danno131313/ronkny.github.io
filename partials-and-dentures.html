Partials and Dentures in Bellingham

Here at Bellingham Smiles, we are proud to offer partials for our patients.

Partial dentures are used when a patient still has some viable teeth in his or her mouth. These dentures are replacement teeth that are attached to a gum-colored base. Partial dentures are sometimes connected to a metal framework so that they stay in a person’s mouth. Many are cemented to your teeth so that they don’t move. Some people prefer dentures that are removable.

Partial dentures are used to fill in the areas where there are missing teeth so a patient has a more natural smile. It also helps to keep the remaining teeth in the correct position in their mouth. It is always our goal to give you a smile that you can be proud of!
