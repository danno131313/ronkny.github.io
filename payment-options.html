Payment Options

Dr. Kavanagh is dedicated to providing you with the highest quality dental care that is convenient, easy and affordable. We do this by discussing your treatment fees and each of your available options. We accept Visa, Mastercard, Discover and American Express. Our finance specialist will assist you in filing your insurance paperwork. In addition, we also offer easy, affordable financing.

Insurance help is available

Our friendly and efficient staff will assist you with your insurance information and help you maximize your benefits. We accept payment from all insurance companies. We are currently contracted with:

  • Delta / WDS
  • Regence

Financing

We provide you with convenient financing options to get you the treatment you need.

CareCredit

CareCredit covers your deductibles, co-payments and any fees that are not listed under your current insurance plan. You can have the convenience of 0% interest on financing for up to 12 months or low interest financing from 24 to 60 months!

The Lending Club Patient Solutions

Our practice is pleased to present flexible payment plans through Lending Club Patient Solutions.

Lending Club Patient Solutions offers:

  • Plans with low fixed rates & low monthly payments that never change over the life of the loan
  • Patient-friendly, True No-Interest Plans with no retroactive interest

  • Payments available for Extended Plans, all without impacting your credit score

Membership Program
