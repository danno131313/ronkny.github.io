Prenatal Dental Care

Dental Care for Pregnant Mothers

This is a very exciting time in your life! We congratulate you and want to help you keep your baby and you healthy.

Pregnancy can cause your gums to bleed and to be inflamed. We can help you to keep them in better shape. Do you know that gum disease can affect the health of your unborn child? The bacteria in your mouth is transmissible to your baby. We have all the means to prevent it.

Please, take care of your little cavities before they turn into a big problem, and extra expenses. We have modern equipment to detect cavities in early stages without harmful x-rays.

Soon you will be very busy with your baby, it is time to take care of yourself!
